Detailed Description

Engagix is your AI-powered LinkedIn post optimizer. Paste any post and instantly get a comprehensive engagement score across 5 key dimensions: Hook Strength, Readability, Call-to-Action, Emotional Impact, and Format Quality.


Engagement Scorer: Get a 0-100 score with a beautiful ring UI showing exactly where your post excels and where it needs work.


Post Optimizer: AI rewrites your post for higher engagement, with a detailed list of every change and the reasoning behind it.


A/B Variants: Generate 3 different versions of your post with unique angles — bold, story-driven, data-backed — and compare scores side by side.


Hook Rewriter: Transform weak opening lines using 5 proven hook styles: Question, Bold Statement, Story, Statistic, and Contrarian.


Viral Predictor: Get a 1-10 viral potential score with detailed factor analysis showing what drives (or kills) shareability.


LinkedIn Integration: Works directly on LinkedIn with injected buttons. See your score ring without leaving the feed. Export as Markdown, TXT, or PDF.
